About me

I am a 26 year old portuguese, bicycle addicted with a simple goal in life: to be happy and free from fear.

I love technology and video games and I am fascinated by science, nature and the human brain. I studied Mechanical Engineering only to find out, on my first job, that my real passion and interest is Programming.

After three years of employment and learning in a great company, I needed change. I am now preparing for a new phase of knowledge and adventure, that hopefully will lead to truly amazing projects.

This preparation involves broadening my Computer Science understanding while developing simple projects for both learning and portfolio purposes.
